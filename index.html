Will I have full ownership of my website after it is developed?

Yes, once development is complete and all services have been paid for, you will have full ownership and control over your website, including the source code, domain, and hosting.

How does your development process address my specific business needs?

We start with a deep analysis of your business, competitive environment, and target audience. This helps us create solutions that are not only aesthetically pleasing, but also functional and effective in achieving your business goals.

What guarantees do you provide for your services?

We provide a guarantee for fixing errors and bugs found within a certain period after the site launch. We also offer additional guarantees and support packages so that you feel confident about your site.

Will my website be optimized for search engines (SEO)?

Yes, we implement SEO best practices when developing your website to ensure its visibility in search engines. We also offer additional SEO optimization and promotion services to improve your website's position in search results.

What if my needs change during development?

We understand that needs may change, and we are flexible in our approach to development. You can discuss changes with us at any time, and we will try to incorporate them into the project by providing an updated schedule and cost estimate.

What technologies do you use to develop websites?

We use modern and proven technologies to develop websites, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Angular, PHP, and WordPress. The choice of technologies depends on the specifics of your project and your preferences.

How ​​can I track the progress of my website development?

We provide transparency in the development process by providing regular progress reports and access to demo versions of your site. You will be able to see how the work is progressing and make your comments and edits at any stage.

What happens if I am not satisfied with the result?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We work closely with you at every stage of development to ensure that the final product meets your expectations. If you are not satisfied with something, we are ready to make the necessary adjustments to ensure your satisfaction.
